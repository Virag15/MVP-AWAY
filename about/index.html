How It All Started

The Journey

It started with a road trip that ran out of space. Bags tied to the roof with bungee cords, gear soaked in a sudden downpour, and a frustrating lack of quality options in the Indian market. That's when the AWAY team decided to build something better.

We spent months studying global leaders in rooftop cargo, then engineered products specifically for Indian roads, weather, and vehicles. The result — aerodynamic roofboxes, waterproof roofbags, and universal-fit roofracks that rival the best in the world, at a price point that makes sense for Indian adventurers.

Today, with over 10,000 happy customers and 24 authorized dealers across India, AWAY is the name that adventure enthusiasts trust when they hit the road.
