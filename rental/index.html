Terms & Conditions

Eligibility

  • Renter must be 18 years or older with a valid ID.
  • Vehicle must have a compatible roof rail system or crossbars.

Rental Period

  • Minimum rental period is 1 day.
  • Extensions are subject to availability and must be requested 24 hours before the end date.

Security Deposit

  • A refundable security deposit is required at the time of booking.
  • Deposit will be refunded within 7 business days after the roofbox is returned in good condition.
  • Damages, missing parts, or late returns may result in partial or full forfeiture of the deposit.

Installation

  • Installation and removal must be performed at an authorized AWAY dealer.
  • Self-installation voids all liability coverage.

Load Capacity

  • Do not exceed the maximum load capacity specified for the roofbox model.
  • AWAY is not liable for damages caused by overloading.

Liability

  • Renter is responsible for the roofbox during the rental period.
  • Any damage, theft, or loss must be reported within 24 hours.
  • AWAY is not liable for damage to the vehicle or its contents during the rental.

Refund Policy

  • Cancellations made 48+ hours before start date: full refund of rental charges.
  • Cancellations within 48 hours: 50% refund of rental charges.
  • No refund for no-shows or cancellations after the start date.
