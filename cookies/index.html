Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, track your activity, and provide a better user experience.

How We Use Cookies

AWAY uses cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your shopping cart items
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Personalize your experience and show relevant content
  • Improve our website performance and functionality
  • Deliver targeted advertising based on your interests

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Purpose: Required for basic site functionality

Examples: Session management, authentication, shopping cart

Duration: Session or up to 1 year

Note: These cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the site to work.

Analytics Cookies

Purpose: Help us understand how visitors use our site

Examples: Google Analytics, page views, bounce rate, traffic sources

Duration: Up to 2 years

We use this data to improve our website and user experience.

Marketing Cookies

Purpose: Track your browsing habits for personalized advertising

Examples: Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, retargeting campaigns

Duration: Up to 1 year

These cookies require your consent and can be disabled.

Functional Cookies

Purpose: Remember your preferences and settings

Examples: Language preference, currency selection, recently viewed products

Duration: Session or up to 1 year

Enhance your experience by remembering your choices.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies

Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of specific tracking services:

  • Google Analytics: Browser Add-on
  • Interest-Based Ads: Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Facebook Ads: Manage preferences in your Facebook account settings

Do Not Track

Some browsers support "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. However, there is no industry standard for responding to DNT. We currently do not respond to DNT signals.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Blocking or deleting cookies may affect your experience:

  • You may need to log in repeatedly
  • Your shopping cart may not save items
  • Website preferences won't be remembered
  • Some features may not function properly
  • Content may not be personalized to your interests

Other Tracking Technologies

Web Beacons (Pixels)

Small transparent images embedded in web pages or emails to track opens, clicks, and user behavior.

Local Storage

Browser storage that allows us to save data locally on your device for improved performance and offline functionality.

Session Storage

Temporary storage that expires when you close your browser, used for session-specific data.
