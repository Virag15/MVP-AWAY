Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers

AWAY is India's premium rooftop cargo systems brand. We design and manufacture high-quality roofboxes, roofbags, roofracks, and crossbars engineered for adventure and built to last. Our products are crafted to withstand Indian road and weather conditions while looking great on any vehicle.
All AWAY products are designed in India and manufactured using premium-grade materials. Our roofboxes use automotive-grade ABS plastic with UV-resistant coating, and our roofracks are built from aircraft-grade aluminum alloy for maximum strength with minimal weight.
AWAY products are available through our network of 24+ authorized dealers across India. You can find the nearest dealer on our Dealer Locator page. You can also purchase directly from our website with free shipping on orders over ₹10,000.
Yes! We offer a rental program for roofboxes. This is perfect for one-off road trips or if you want to try before you buy. Contact us or visit an authorized dealer for rental availability and pricing in your area.
We offer express shipping across India. Most orders are dispatched within 24-48 hours. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. Metro cities usually receive orders within 3-4 business days.
Yes, we offer free shipping on all orders over ₹10,000. For orders below this amount, a flat shipping fee of ₹499 applies. All orders include tracking so you can monitor your shipment in real-time.
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking ID via email and SMS. You can use the "Track Your Order" section at the top of this page, or click the tracking link in your email to see real-time delivery status.
We accept all major payment methods including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, UPI, Google Pay, and net banking. All payments are processed through a secure 256-bit encrypted gateway. We also offer Cash on Delivery (COD) on select products.
AWAY roofboxes are designed with a universal mounting system compatible with most factory-installed roof rails and aftermarket crossbars. Check the product page for specific vehicle compatibility, or contact us with your car make and model for confirmation.
Our roofboxes have a maximum load capacity of 50-75 kg depending on the model. The 540L Roofbox supports up to 75 kg. Always check your vehicle's roof load rating as well — the lower of the two limits applies.
Yes, all AWAY roofboxes feature IP67-rated sealing with dual-side weatherproof gaskets. Our roofbags use heavy-duty PVC-coated fabric with welded seams rated at 10,000mm waterproof. Your gear stays dry in any weather condition.
Our roofboxes are aerodynamically designed to minimize wind resistance. Expect a 2-5% increase in fuel consumption at highway speeds when loaded, which is significantly lower than open roof racks or non-aerodynamic alternatives.
We offer a 30-day easy return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. The product must be unused, in original packaging, and with all accessories included. Return shipping is free.
All AWAY products come with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. This includes the locking mechanism, hinges, mounting hardware, and structural integrity. Normal wear and tear, misuse, or modifications are not covered.
Use the contact form below or email us at support@awayindia.com with your order ID and a description of the issue. For warranty claims, please include photos of the defect. Our team will respond within 24 hours with next steps.
Yes! All AWAY roofboxes come with detailed installation instructions and all necessary mounting hardware. Installation typically takes 15-20 minutes with basic tools. We also have video guides on our YouTube channel for step-by-step guidance.
Yes, roofboxes require crossbars for mounting. If your vehicle has factory roof rails, you can add AWAY crossbars for a perfect fit. If your car doesn't have roof rails, we offer door-clamp crossbar solutions. Check the crossbars section in our shop for compatible options.
Absolutely. All our authorized dealers offer professional installation services. Some dealers include free installation with purchase. Find a dealer near you on our Dealer Locator page and contact them directly for installation availability.
AWAY roofboxes are tested and rated for speeds up to 130 km/h when properly installed and loaded within the weight limit. We recommend keeping speeds under 120 km/h for optimal safety and fuel efficiency, especially when the roofbox is fully loaded.
