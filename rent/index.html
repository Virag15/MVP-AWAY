Rental FAQs

Everything You Need To Know

Every rental includes the roofbox or roofbag, mounting hardware, free professional installation at the dealer, and a protective cargo mat. Crossbars are available as an add-on if your vehicle doesn't have them.
Rental rates start at ₹1,999 for 1-5 days for roofboxes. Weekly and monthly rates offer significant discounts with daily rates as low as ₹349/day. A refundable security deposit (₹4,000–₹5,000) is collected at pickup and returned upon safe return.
Normal wear and tear is expected and covered. For significant damage, repair costs are deducted from your security deposit. We inspect every unit upon return and document any issues transparently.
Yes, extensions are available subject to availability. Contact us or your dealer at least 24 hours before your scheduled return date. Extended days are billed at the standard daily rate for your product.
